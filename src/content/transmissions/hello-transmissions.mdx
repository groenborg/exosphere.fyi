This is the first Transmission. There will be others.

We started Exosphere because we wanted to build software that *feels* like
something — not just runs. This blog is the long-form sibling of that project:
a place to put the thinking that doesn't fit on a homepage or in a tweet.

## What you'll find here

A short, honest list:

- **Postcards from inside projects.** Things we're shipping, mid-flight.
- **Small ideas.** Half-formed, sometimes wrong, always sincere.
- **Field notes on tools.** Why we picked this stack, what we'd swap.
- **The occasional weekend experiment** that didn't make it into a product.

<Eyebrow>Side note</Eyebrow>

We're writing in **MDX**, which means a Transmission can be a paragraph,
a working demo, or a small interactive component. Whatever the idea needs.

> The studio is small on purpose. The writing will be too.

## What's next

A handful of pieces are queued up: a deep-dive on how we built `Helios Control`,
notes on the OKLCH colour system we use across the site, and an honest
post-mortem on a project we shelved. See you in the next one.
