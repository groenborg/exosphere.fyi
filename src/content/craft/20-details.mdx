Nobody writes a review thanking you for the focus ring you fixed on the date
picker, the loading state you tightened, or the error message you rewrote from
*"Something went wrong"* to something a human could act on. They just keep
using the thing, and feel inexplicably good about it.

That's the deal. Details are mostly invisible when they're right and only
visible when they're wrong. You either sign up for that asymmetry or you
don't.
