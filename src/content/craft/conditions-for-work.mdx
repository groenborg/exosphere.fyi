Most software is built in conditions that would embarrass anyone who'd ever
tried to make something well. Pings every six minutes. Status meetings about
the status meetings. Open offices, open Slacks, open calendars, open season.

People do their best work when they're left alone, trusted to deliver, and
respected enough to be told when something isn't working. None of those three
are particularly difficult to provide. It is striking how many places provide
none of them.
