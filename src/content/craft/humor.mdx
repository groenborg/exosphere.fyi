Humor isn't decoration. It's a signal that a team is honest enough to admit
when something is silly, secure enough to be wrong out loud, and present
enough to notice the absurd in the work.

We've worked in places where nobody laughed for months. The work was always
bad. Not coincidentally bad — *causally* bad. Without humor, problems get
quietly tolerated. People stop pointing at the obvious.

So we hire for it, we write for it, and we let it into the product where it
fits.
